Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-26

What we collect

  • Contact details you submit (name, email, phone, city, brokerage, listing URL).
  • Operational message metadata when using the pilot (timestamps, delivery status). No MLS credentials stored in v1.

Why we collect it

  • To set up your pilot and provide the service (replying to buyer inquiries, booking showings).
  • To measure reliability and value (first reply time, showings booked).
  • To communicate with you about the service.

Lawful basis & consent

By submitting the pilot form you consent to being contacted about the pilot. SMS/WhatsApp include “STOP” to opt out (CASL-compliant transactional messaging).

Processors

We use reputable vendors for hosting and messaging (e.g., Netlify, Twilio, email providers). They process data on our behalf under contractual safeguards.

Retention

Pilot submissions are retained while your pilot is active and then for up to 12 months unless you request deletion sooner.

Your rights

Request access, correction, or deletion at founder@recopilot.app.

Security

We store minimal PII, enforce access controls, and use HTTPS everywhere.

Contact

Questions? founder@recopilot.app
